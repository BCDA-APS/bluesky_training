APS Bluesky Training#

The APS Bluesky Training repository [1] demonstrates use of the Bluesky framework at a typical APS beamline. Its bluesky/ directory [2] also serves as a template for new beamline installations.

[1]

repository: BCDA-APS/bluesky_training

[2]

bluesky/ directory: new beamline template
